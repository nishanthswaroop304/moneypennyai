APR details 
* Annual Percentage Rate (APR) for Purchases: 21.24% to 29.24%, based on your creditworthiness. These APRs will vary with the market based on the Prime Rate.
* APR for Balance Transfers: 21.24% to 29.24%, based on your creditworthiness, for transfers completed within 2 months from date of account opening. These APRs will vary with the market based on the Prime Rate.
* APR for Cash Advances: 29.99%. This APR will vary with the market based on the Prime Rate.b

Fees details 
* Annual Fee: $95

* Cash Advance: Either $10 or 5% of the amount of each cash advance, whichever is greater.
* Foreign Purchase Transaction: None
* Late Payment: Up to $41
